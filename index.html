Well.. It seems like I'll be making a blog.

This is something I've been wanting to do for a really long time now, this blog will be a way for me to share some opinions on some new technologies and projects I'm working on. Let's start with a small Q&A!

Goal

The goal of this blog is to teach you something, and to share some kind of knowledge.

Who am I?

I'm a passionate Developer, a few years ago I picked Java as my religion and now I'm trying to learn everything around it. I'm also an avid reader, and I try to read as many books as I can. I firmly believe that reading is one of the best way to gain some new insights.

Why not a CMS?

This blog is hosted on github, why don't I use a CMS? Am I so stingy that I don't want to pay for some wordpress hosting? Well... Not really. I want to be able to write this blog from within my IDE, this IDE is like my home/safe zone and I feel like it's way easier to write here than on some CMS.

Content

The content on this blog will be divided in three types:

  • Projects: Here I'll discuss the projects I'm working on. (Ex. this blog)
  • Blog: You'll find mostly short articles about a technology I tried and things I saw or experienced.
  • Books: I'm an avid reader and I plan on sharing my thoughts about the books I read.

I'll try to diversify the content as much as possible. Every content block will also have some notes that I want to share, they may not seem interesting now, but once I think I'll be able to make good use of them once I've made a few blog posts.

This blog is ugly

For now..
But you'll see, this will become pretty pleasing for the eyes. Or not. (I'm not a Front-end dev after all...)
